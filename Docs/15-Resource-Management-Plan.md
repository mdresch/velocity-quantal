## Summary

Resource planning focuses on allocating contributor time, mentors, and any required tools. Emphasize low-cost, high-learning contributors and clear role definitions.

## Key Practices

- Define roles and expected weekly hours per pilot.
- Maintain a lightweight RACI for each pilot.
- Encourage shared responsibilities for tooling and reporting.
