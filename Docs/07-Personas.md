## Executive Summary

This Personas document describes the primary user types for the program and their goals, motivations, and success criteria. Use these personas to guide opportunity selection, pilot design, and communication strategies.

## Personas

- Individual Entrepreneur
  - Description: Self-starter seeking low-barrier ways to generate initial capital using time and skills.
  - Goals: Find actionable ideas, run quick pilots, and generate initial revenue.
  - Success metrics: number of pilots executed, capital generated, skill improvements.

- Market Analyst
  - Description: Research-focused persona that curates and scores opportunities using market signals.
  - Goals: Surface high-probability ideas and prioritize by trend and feasibility.
  - Success metrics: quality of curated list, pilot hit-rate.

- Mentor / Advisor
  - Description: Experienced practitioner who validates ideas, advises pilot owners, and provides introductions.
  - Goals: Improve pilot success rates and accelerate learning.
  - Success metrics: mentor feedback adoption, pilot success improvements.

- Program Manager
  - Description: Oversees portfolio of pilots, reporting, and stakeholder coordination.
  - Goals: Track progress, manage risks, and produce executive reporting.
  - Success metrics: timely reports, capital aggregated, stakeholder satisfaction.

## Using Personas

- Tailor pilot onboarding materials and templates to the Individual Entrepreneur and Mentor personas.
- Provide Market Analysts with data exports and filters to support prioritization.
- Build dashboards and executive summaries for the Program Manager and Sponsors.

## Validation

Conduct persona validation workshops during pilot planning to refine needs and adjust pilot design. Update personas after initial pilots based on observed behavior and feedback.
