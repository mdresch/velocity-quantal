## Executive Summary

This Stakeholder Register lists the primary stakeholders for the "Opportunities with No Working Capital" program, summarizes their roles and interests, and identifies engagement approaches and contact points. Use this register to guide communications, decision-making, and escalation.

## Stakeholder List

- Sponsor: Executive Sponsor — provides strategic direction, approves funding and scope.
- Project Manager: Program Lead — responsible for delivery, planning, and reporting.
- Core Team: Delivery Team (product lead, analysts, developers, operations) — executes pilots and maintains artifacts.
- Business Sponsors: Functional owners who benefit from outcomes (e.g., Marketing, Sales) — provide subject-matter input and adoption support.
- End Users: Entrepreneurs / individual contributors using the low-capital opportunity framework — primary beneficiaries and feedback providers.
- Mentors / Advisors: External advisors and mentors — provide guidance, validation and introductions.
- Partners & Vendors: Third-party providers (tools, platforms) — supply services or integrations.
- Finance / Legal: Compliance, cost oversight, and contractual review — approve financial controls and legal compliance.

## Stakeholder Attributes (example template)

- Name / Role: e.g., "Executive Sponsor — Jane Doe"
- Interest / Influence: High/Medium/Low
- Engagement Approach: Inform, Consult, Collaborate, or Empower
- Primary Contact: email / slack channel

## Engagement Strategy

- High-influence, high-interest stakeholders (Sponsor, PM) — weekly steering updates; direct escalation channel.
- Core Team — daily/weekly standups and a shared workboard; collaborative decision-making for execution details.
- End users and mentors — monthly demo sessions, feedback surveys, and pilot retrospectives.
- Finance/Legal — milestone-based review and formal approvals prior to rollout.

## Maintenance and Review

The register should be reviewed monthly during the early pilot phase and after major milestones. Record changes with date, reason, and approver.
