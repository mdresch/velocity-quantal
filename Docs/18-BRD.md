## Overview

This BRD summarizes the business needs, high-level requirements, and acceptance criteria for the program. Detailed appendices (user stories, business case, stakeholder register, templates) are provided in the appendices index.

## High-Level Requirements

- Curated opportunity repository with metadata and feasibility notes.
- Pilot execution framework with templates and KPI capture.
- Reporting dashboard for program-level KPIs and sponsor summaries.

## Acceptance Criteria

- All pilots include documented metrics and a retrospective.
- Dashboard shows aggregated capital generated and top-performing pilots.
