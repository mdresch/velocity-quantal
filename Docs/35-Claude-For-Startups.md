## Program Overview

Claude for Startups provides API credits, elevated rate limits, and curated founder events/workshops to help early-stage companies build with Anthropic's Claude models. The program is typically available through Anthropic's VC partners and is focused on enabling rapid product work with technical support and community access.

## Eligibility

- Program access is commonly granted to early-stage startups backed by Anthropic partner venture firms. Check with your investors for a partner link or program application.
- Eligibility requirements and partner lists may change; verify on Anthropic's site or via your VC partner.

## Key Benefits

- Free API credits to experiment and build without immediate cost pressure.
- Premium/public-tier rate limits to support development and scaling during early growth.
- Educational resources: best practices, technical documentation, and implementation guides.
- Networking: invitations to exclusive startup-focused events, workshops, and founder meetups.
- Direct support: opportunities for office hours, technical guidance, and access to Anthropic contacts.

## Anthology Fund & Strategic Partnerships

- Anthropic's Anthology Fund and partner initiatives (e.g., Menlo Ventures collaboration) have provided structured credit programs (example: $25k in Claude credits for eligible startups) and additional venture support—often focused on domains like healthcare, education, and scientific research.

## Program mechanics & verification

1. Confirm partner affiliation (investor or accelerator) and request the program application link.
2. Complete the application with corporate email, website, and funding/partner details.
3. Anthropic or partner reviewers may request verification (Crunchbase, investor contact, or partner confirmation).
4. If approved, Anthropic provisions credits/rate limits and provides onboarding resources.

## FAQ (short)
- Who is eligible? Typically startups backed by Anthropic partner VCs or accelerators; check your investor for a partner link.
- What do I get? API credits, elevated rate limits, educational resources, events, and potential support channels.
- How do I apply? Work through your VC partner or the Anthropic program page; provide company and funding details.

## Recommended evidence checklist for applications
- Corporate email and workspace domain.
- Company website or public profile (Crunchbase, PitchBook, AngelList).
- Verification of VC partner or accelerator affiliation.
- Short justification: product use case and how Claude will be used (primary dependency vs. embedded feature).
