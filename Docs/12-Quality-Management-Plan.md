## Purpose

The QMP defines quality expectations for pilot execution, data collection, and deliverables. It outlines assurance and control activities, metrics, and continuous improvement approaches.

## Quality Objectives

- Ensure pilot metrics are collected consistently and accurately.
- Deliverable templates meet the minimum acceptance criteria for handover.
- Apply simple QA checks on dashboard data and reports.

## Assurance Activities

- Pilot readiness checklist and acceptance criteria for each pilot.
- Data validation steps and sampling for reported KPIs.
- Periodic quality reviews and a lessons-learned log.
