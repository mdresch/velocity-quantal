## Executive Summary

This Communications Plan defines audiences, cadence, channels, and responsibilities for the "Opportunities with No Working Capital" program. Clear, timely communication will enable fast feedback loops during pilots and ensure sponsors and stakeholders remain informed for decision-making.

## Audiences

- Executive Sponsor(s)
- Project Manager and Core Team
- Business Sponsors (functional owners)
- End Users / Pilot Participants
- Mentors and Advisors
- Finance / Legal / Compliance

## Communication Channels & Cadence

- Steering updates: bi-weekly executive summary email to Sponsors (owner: PM).
- Core team: weekly standup and shared workboard (owner: Team Lead).
- Pilot updates: short demo + retrospective at the end of each pilot (owner: Pilot Owner).
- Monthly program dashboard: automated report showing capital generated, pilot status, and KPIs (owner: PM/Reporting lead).
- Ad-hoc escalation: direct Slack/Teams channel for urgent issues (owner: PM).

## Message Types

- Status: progress against milestones, risks, and blockers.
- Decision: scope changes, funding requests, approvals.
- Learnings: pilot outcomes, lessons learned, and recommended next steps.
- Requests: asks for resources, approvals, or participation.

## Roles & Responsibilities

- Project Manager: central coordination, stakeholder updates, and reporting cadence.
- Pilot Owners: execute pilots, prepare demo, and capture metrics/lessons.
- Sponsors: provide approvals and high-level guidance.
- Mentors: provide expert feedback and validation sessions.

## Communication Templates

- Executive summary template: 3 bullets — (1) status, (2) key risk/issue, (3) request/decision.
- Pilot demo agenda: objective, metrics, what worked, what didn't, next steps.

## Review and Maintenance

The Communications Plan will be reviewed quarterly and after each major program milestone. Update channels and cadence based on stakeholder feedback and scale of operations.
