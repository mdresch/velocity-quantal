## Offer Summary

Atlassian for Startups provides eligible early-stage companies up to 50 seats free for 12 months across a curated set of Atlassian Cloud products plus select partner perks. The program is designed to help founders scale from MVP to IPO with tooling, CSM support, and community access.

Key highlights:
- Up to 50 free seats for 12 months (program duration per eligible company).
- Included products (examples): Jira Premium, Confluence Premium, Bitbucket Premium, Compass Premium, Jira Product Discovery Premium (10 creator seats + unlimited contributors), Loom Business + AI, and CI/CD pipelines where included.
- Startup-focused support: Dedicated Customer Success Managers and community enablement.
- AI teammate: Rovo (Atlassian's AI extensions) to surface work, automate tasks, and build custom agents.

## Eligibility (summary)
- Not currently an existing Atlassian paid customer.
- VC-funded or associated with a partner accelerator/incubator.
- Have not raised more than US$10 million in external funding.
- Application review may request verification (Crunchbase/Pitchbook, proof of funding, or partner referral).

## What you get (program mechanics)
- Jira Premium – agile planning and development (up to 50 seats).
- Confluence Premium – knowledge management and startup templates (up to 50 seats).
- Loom Business + AI – screen recording and editing (up to 50 seats).
- Jira Product Discovery Premium – idea prioritization (10 free creators, unlimited contributors).
- Compass Premium – service health and delivery performance (up to 50 full seats, unlimited basic seats).
- Bitbucket Premium – source control and pipelines (up to 50 seats).
- Note: Atlassian Marketplace apps are not included by default; offerings may change over time.

## Application & verification
1. Apply via the Atlassian for Startups application form and provide corporate email, website, and funding/partner details.
2. Atlassian reviews each application and may request verification (e.g., Crunchbase profile, investor contact, or partner confirmation).
3. If approved, Atlassian provisions the program for a 12-month period.

## Duration & renewal
- The initial program period is 12 months per eligible startup. Before the end of the period you may choose to renew or upgrade to paid tiers.
- If expanding beyond 50 seats (or 10 creators for Product Discovery) you will be offered paid pricing for additional seats.

## Support & community
- Dedicated Customer Success Managers provide onboarding and enablement.
- Access to Atlassian Community Startups group and partner enablement resources.

## FAQ (short)
- Is my company eligible? You must not be a current Atlassian paid customer, be VC-funded or partner-affiliated, and have raised ≤US$10M.
- How does verification work? Atlassian reviews applications and may ask for documentation or partner confirmation.
- What happens after 12 months? You may renew or transition to paid tiers; Atlassian will provide guidance and quotes.
- Are Marketplace apps included? No, marketplace apps are not automatically included in the program.

## Recommended evidence checklist for applications and partner submissions
- Corporate email and workspace domain.
- Company website or public profile (Crunchbase, PitchBook, AngelList).
- Confirmation of funding or partner affiliation (investor contact or accelerator reference).
- Short justification: how Atlassian tooling + Rovo AI will accelerate product delivery and operations.
