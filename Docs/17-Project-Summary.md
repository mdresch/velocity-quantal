## Executive Summary

This Project Summary consolidates the core purpose, objectives, and recommended approach for the program: identify and validate business opportunities that require no monetary upfront investment, execute small pilots, measure outcomes, and reinvest proceeds to scale the most promising initiatives.

## Objectives

- Identify 10 candidate opportunities and run 3 pilots in the initial program phase.
- Generate initial capital and achieve measurable ROI on selected pilots.
